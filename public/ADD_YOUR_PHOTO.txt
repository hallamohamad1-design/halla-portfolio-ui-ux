To add your photo to the About Me panel:

1. Take the photo you shared in chat (the one with the hijab and dark background)
2. Save it as: halla-photo.jpg
3. Place it in this folder: portofolio/public/halla-photo.jpg

The About panel will automatically show it in the circular avatar.
If the file is missing, it shows "HM" initials as fallback.
